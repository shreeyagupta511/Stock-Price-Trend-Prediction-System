StockTrend

How The System Works

Linear Regression

Detects the overall stock price trend using a best-fit line and used to fit a curve that minimizes prediction error.

Numerical Differentiation

Measures rate of change in prices to identify market momentum and used to identify the trend direction by computing rate of change.

Euler Method

Uses recent price change to estimate the next-day stock value and used to approximate future price using differential equation modeling.

Interpolation

Fits a smooth curve to estimate intermediate and future stock values and used to estimate stock price values at unknown points using known data.

The system will analyze real time stock trends, detect volatility, and predict the next-day price.
